Checklist Feature Overview

Feature Objective:

The checklist feature is designed to display a list of useful knowledge and preparation items on the lesson page for students to know before starting the lesson. This feature aims to improve learning efficiency by helping students prepare and engage smoothly with the lesson content.

Feature Description:

Instructors can create and edit checklist for each lesson, allowing them to add or edit items as needed. This checklist is embedded within the lesson page, enabling students to effectively prepare for their learning tasks and track their progress.

UX/UI Considerations:

The design of the checklist feature focuses on simplicity and ease of use, ensuring that users can navigate and utilize the feature without unnecessary complexity. Angular Material components were used to maintain consistency with the overall design of the GuruFyre platform.
